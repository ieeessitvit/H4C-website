ABOUT HACK4CAUSE

IEEE-SSIT would like to bring Hack4Cause, a 24 hour hackathon
which aims to bring creativity and innovation
among
students Hack4Cause brings students from different domains of
engineering together to build design and use the
latest technology to hack into real world problems.The dates of the
event are 10th and 11th of March.The main
motive behind Hack-4-Cause is to bring a change in the thinking
perspective of students in crtical industrial
situations
